Employability Prediction System

Western Mindanao State University

Zamboanga City, Philippines.

About

The proposed system aims to predict the employability of future graduates of the College of Computing Studies based on their grades from 1st year to 3rd year college, through the use of the proposed system, the student will have a knowledge in which subject they should improve more as it also has a recommendation in which subjects you need to enhance to meet the job market of the IT industry.

Instruction

  1. To start click on the Start Prediction labeled below
  2. The system will request the student to input grades in respective major courses
  3. It then output the particular job the student will end up in the future
  4. Also outputs the top 5 major courses the student should improve to secure an IT related job
